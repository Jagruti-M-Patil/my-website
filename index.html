Hello! I’m Jagruti.

I’m a 4th-year Ph.D. student at IIT Gandhinagar in the Medical Ultrasound Engineering Lab (MUSE). My work focuses on biomedical ultrasound imaging and quantitative ultrasound for soft-tissue characterization.

I develop and evaluate methods based on envelope statistics (e.g., Nakagami and Burr), H-scan imaging, and elastography-related metrics to quantify microstructural changes in tissue. I completed my B.E. in Biomedical Engineering from L.D. College of Engineering, Ahmedabad (2021), and I’m motivated by research that can support earlier diagnosis and improved outcomes.
